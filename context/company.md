## What we do

ClickUp is a work management platform that combines project management, chat, docs, time tracking, calendars, and automation in one application. It includes AI-powered agents that automate tasks, answer questions, and manage workflows across different types of work.

## Who we serve

Teams and companies of all sizes, from startups to Fortune 500 enterprises. The site highlights use cases for projects, marketing, product and engineering, IT, HR, and leadership teams.

## How we're different

ClickUp positions itself as a unified replacement for fragmented tool stacks (Slack, Google Drive, Jira, Notion, Monday, Asana, etc.) rather than a single-purpose tool. The core differentiator is combining software, AI agents, and humans with full context in one workspace, with emphasis on the AI agents operating across all workflows and tools.
